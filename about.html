Our story

About SassaGold

Built for clarity, safety, and simplicity — a travel companion that stays out of your way until you need it.

Why SassaGold exists

Travel apps were
making travel harder

Most travel apps are overloaded with features you don't need, cluttered with ads, and slow to open when you're in a hurry. When you're on the road — especially in an unfamiliar area — you need reliable information, fast.

SassaGold was built to solve that. It strips away everything unnecessary and focuses on three things: knowing what's nearby, staying safe, and getting help when you need it.

No ads. No account. No clutter. Just the tools that matter.

✦ Local points of interest — fuel, food, hospitals, parking
✦ Emergency numbers & tools — available offline
✦ Five languages — switch instantly, remembered forever
✦ Offline-first design — works without signal

Design philosophy

Scandinavian simplicity

Four principles that guide every decision we make.

⬜

Minimalism first

Every element on screen earns its place. If it doesn't help you travel better, it doesn't belong in the app.

📵

Zero ads, zero tracking

We don't monetize your data or attention. No ads, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Your privacy is not a feature — it's a baseline.

🔋

Reliability above all

An app that fails when you need it most is worse than no app. SassaGold is built to work in the conditions travelers actually face.

🌱

Built to last

No feature bloat, no endless chasing of trends. A focused tool that does a few things exceptionally well and keeps doing them.
