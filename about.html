Our story

About Where Am I

Built for clarity, safety, and simplicity — a ride companion that stays out of your way until you need it.

Why Where Am I exists

Rider apps were
making riding harder

Most apps for bikers are overloaded with features you don't need, cluttered with ads, and slow to open when you're on the road — especially in an unfamiliar area or a foreign country. When you're riding, you need reliable information, fast.

Where Am I was built to solve that. It strips away everything unnecessary and focuses on three things: knowing what's nearby for riders, staying safe, and getting help when you need it.

No ads. No account. No clutter. Just the tools that matter on the road.

✦ Biker POIs — fuel, moto service, biker cafés, rest stops
✦ Emergency numbers & roadside assistance — available offline
✦ Nine languages — switch instantly, remembered forever
✦ Offline-first design — works beyond cell coverage
Who it's for

Built for riders on the road

Whether you're on a solo tour, group ride, or pushing into remote territory.

🏍️

Solo Riders

Design philosophy

Scandinavian simplicity

Four principles that guide every decision we make.

⬜

Minimalism first

Every element on screen earns its place. If it doesn't help you travel better, it doesn't belong in the app.

📵

Zero ads, zero tracking

We don't monetize your data or attention. No ads, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Your privacy is not a feature — it's a baseline.

🔋

Reliability above all

An app that fails when you need it most is worse than no app. Where Am I is built to work in the conditions riders actually face — remote roads, no signal, full gear on.

🌱

Built to last

No feature bloat, no endless chasing of trends. A focused tool that does a few things exceptionally well and keeps doing them.
