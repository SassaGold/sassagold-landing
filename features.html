SOS & Emergency

A dedicated emergency screen that puts help one tap away. Find the nearest services, share your exact location, and access first-aid guidance — all without an internet connection.

  • One-tap call to 112 (international emergency)
  • Nearest hospitals, police, fire stations & pharmacies
  • Location sharing via SMS or WhatsApp
  • Torch / flash screen for signalling
  • Step-by-step first-aid for motorcycle accidents

Offline‑Friendly

Remote roads often have spotty or no signal. Where Am I is designed from the ground up to work without a reliable connection. Essential data is cached on your device so you stay informed even when riding beyond cell coverage.

  • Emergency tools always available offline
  • Previously loaded POIs cached locally
  • Language settings stored on device
  • No mobile data required for core features

  • Glanceable design for use while on the road
  • Scandinavian simplicity — nothing superfluous
