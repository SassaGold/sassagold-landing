Privacy Policy — SassaGold

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data SassaGold collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We have written it to be clear and easy to understand.

1. Who We Are

SassaGold is a travel companion application. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the SassaGold team. You can reach us at support@sassagold.com.

2. Data We Collect

SassaGold is built on a principle of minimal data collection. We do not collect personal data, do not track your identity, and do not share information with third parties.

The app only processes the minimum data required to function, including:

  • Language preference — stored locally on your device so the app remembers your chosen language.
  • Cached content — local points of interest and other app data cached on your device for offline use.

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information through the app itself.

3. How We Use Data

The minimal data described above is used solely to provide core app functionality — specifically, remembering your language setting and enabling offline access to content. It is never used for advertising, profiling, or shared with any third party.

4. Data Storage

All data processed by the app is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store personal information from app users.

5. Third-Party Services

SassaGold does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. The Google Play Store may collect data as per Google's Privacy Policy when you download or update the app.

6. Children's Privacy

SassaGold does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features designed to attract or collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is generally no personal information for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have questions about data stored on your device, you can clear the app's local storage through your device settings.

If you believe we hold any personal information about you and wish to exercise your rights (access, correction, deletion), please contact us at support@sassagold.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical precautions to protect any information processed by the app. Since we do not transmit personal data to external servers, the primary security consideration is the security of your own device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@sassagold.com
