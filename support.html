Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Email us at support@sassagold.com

How do I change the language?
Open the app and tap Settings, then select Language. You'll see a list of all supported languages — tap one to switch instantly. SassaGold supports Norwegian, Danish, Swedish, Dutch, and English. Your choice is saved automatically and remembered every time you open the app.
Why am I not seeing certain points of interest?
Points of interest are updated automatically when your device has an internet connection. If you're in an area with limited or no coverage, some POIs may not be visible until the app syncs. Previously loaded POIs are cached locally and will still appear even offline. Try refreshing the list when you regain connectivity.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. SassaGold is designed to work with limited or no connectivity. Core features — including all emergency tools and previously cached local information — remain available offline. You can access emergency numbers, view saved POIs, and use the app in your chosen language without any internet connection.
How often does the app update its content?
The app checks for new content and improvements automatically in the background, whenever a connection is available. You don't need to do anything — SassaGold handles updates silently. There's no need to manually refresh or reinstall the app to get the latest information.
Do I need to create an account?
No. SassaGold requires no account, no sign-up, and no login. You install it, open it, and it works. Your language preference and cached data are stored locally on your device — not on any server.
What emergency numbers does the app show?
SassaGold shows local emergency numbers for the region you're in, including police, ambulance, fire brigade, coast guard, and the pan-European emergency number 112. The information is curated for the Scandinavian and European regions the app currently supports.
Is my location data shared or stored?
No. SassaGold does not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any server. The app may use your device's location to show nearby points of interest — but this is processed entirely on your device and never sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Which devices and Android versions are supported?
SassaGold is designed for Android devices running Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later. The app is optimized for modern smartphones and tablets. iOS support is not currently planned, but may be considered in the future.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at support@sassagold.com with a description of the issue or your idea. We read every message and genuinely appreciate feedback — it helps make the app better for everyone.
Does SassaGold have any ads or in-app purchases?
No. SassaGold is completely free, contains no advertisements, and has no in-app purchases. There are no premium tiers, subscriptions, or hidden costs of any kind.
How do I use the emergency tools?
Tap the Emergency tab at the bottom of the app. You'll see local emergency numbers (police, ambulance, fire brigade, coast guard) for the region you're currently in. Tap any number to dial directly. The emergency tab is always accessible, even when the rest of the app is offline.
Can I use SassaGold without enabling location / GPS?
Yes. Location permission is entirely optional. Without it, the app displays curated points of interest for the region you have selected in Settings rather than automatically showing nearby places. All other features — emergency numbers, language switching, and offline content — work fully without location access.
How do I update the app to the latest version?
SassaGold updates are delivered through the Google Play Store. If you have automatic updates enabled, your device will update the app in the background. Otherwise, open the Play Store, search for SassaGold, and tap Update if one is available.
Will reinstalling the app delete my settings?
Uninstalling SassaGold clears locally cached content and your language preference. After reinstalling, simply open Settings and choose your preferred language again — the app will re-cache local content the next time you're connected to the internet. No account or backup is required.
What is SassaGold's battery and data usage?
SassaGold is designed to be lightweight. It does not run continuous background services or use push notifications, so battery impact is minimal. Data usage is limited to occasional background syncs when new content is available — typically a few kilobytes per update. No data is consumed when you're using cached content offline.
