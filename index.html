Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?
Open the app's settings menu and select your preferred language from the list. SassaGold supports Norwegian, Danish, Swedish, Dutch, and English — and remembers your choice for future sessions.
Why am I not seeing certain points of interest?
Points of interest are updated automatically when your device has a connection. If you're in an area with limited coverage, some POIs may not be visible until the app syncs. Try refreshing when you regain connectivity.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. SassaGold is designed to work with limited or no connectivity. Core features — including emergency tools and previously cached local information — remain available offline.
How often does the app update?
The app checks for new content and improvements automatically in the background. You don't need to manually trigger updates — SassaGold handles it for you whenever a connection is available.
